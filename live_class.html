Course Overview

Learn Readymade Rangoli – The Easy & Creative Way! By शिल्पा कला
Join our step-by-step, beginner-friendly 10-day Rangoli workshop designed to help you master the art of Readymade and Designer Rangoli with ease.
Each session includes a blend of theory and hands-on practice, making it perfect for learners of all ages!
Workshop Schedule: शिल्पा कला
Day 1: Introduction to the Art of Rangoli Explore the history, cultural significance, and styles of Rangoli across India.
Day 2: Tools & Materials Get to know essential tools, readymade kits, colors, base materials & accessories.
Day 3: Using Stencils the Right Way Learn how to use Rangoli stencils effectively to create clean and consistent designs.
Day 4: Designing with a Theme Discover how to plan and create theme-based Rangolis for weddings, festivals, and events.
Day 5: Application of Readymade Rangoli Master techniques to apply pre-designed Rangoli elements quickly and neatly.
Day 6: DIY Stencil Creation Get creative with custom stencil-making using household items or basic craft tools.
Day 7: Diwali Special – Festival Rangoli Design stunning Diwali-themed Rangolis with multiple layers, motifs, and lamps.
Day 8: Multi-Layer & Gradient Design Learn professional-level Rangoli with layering, shading, and gradient techniques.
Day 9: Commercial Use & Display Understand how to create Rangoli for exhibitions, showrooms, home décor, and gifts.
Day 10: Final Project & Certification Create your final project using all techniques learned, followed by an e-certificate!
